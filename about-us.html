Global Academic Mentors (GAM) began in early 2019 with an honest, simple vision--to provide highly-personalized, professional academic services for students in a simple, streamlined, online format. After teaming up with a small group of professional educators as well as recruiters in regions around the world, GAM has quickly become a popular resource for families to find high-quality academic support in a broad range of academic subjects, with all the convenience and safety of online tutorial at home.
Today, in addition to providing professional resources for academic skills development, our services are expanding to include Academic Mentorship Programs, and new learning opportunities in a wider range of subjects, including Small-Group Classes, Self-Designed Project-Based Learning Groups, Goal Setting and Benchmarking, Distance Monitoring Reports for busy parents,Academic Counseling & Parenting Resources for Academic Success, and more.
