ACADEMIC MENTORING

GAM understands the importance of academic success. At the same time, to be successful in the future, students need a global outlook, as well as a 21st century skill set not taught in traditional school programs . GAM's unique Tutoring & Mentoring program gives students the best of academic advising and real-world, practical, academic mentoring. Experienced tutors are available to ensure your student is excelling in all subject areas. Students are engaged with topics that stimulate their personal growth, as well as develop their cross-cultural awareness and intercultural communication skills.
