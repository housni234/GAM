With the world's 9th largest economy, Italy is a leading country for anyone interested in design, fine cuisine, fashion, luxury cars, engines, and wine, and the Italian language is the key to success in these sectors. Academic Mentoring: SCIENCE
Level: K to 12 University+
Schedule: Flexible
Focus: Flexible
Instructor: Rafael A. Quintero Arcaya, PhD
CHEMISTRY LEVEL: INTRODUCTORY 9-12 Schedule: Flexible Instructor: Rafael A. Quintero Arcaya, PhD
Stoichiometric Relationship (5h)
Atoms & Molecules, Elements, Compounds, Substance and Mixtures. States of a Pure Substance. The Mole, the 12C Mass Standard, Relative atomic and Molecular Masses. Avogadro´s Number. Empirical and Molecular Formula.
Atomic Structure (5h)
Subatomic particles: Proton, Neutron and the Electrón, Atoms and Isotopes. The Mass Spectrometer. Emission Spectra. H-atom Emission Spectrum. The H-atom Energy Level Diagram, Sublevels and Orbitals. Pauli Exclusion Principle.
Periodicity (5h)
The Periodic Table and the H-atom Energy Level Diagram. Groups and Periods. Position of Metals, Nonmetals and Metalloids. Periodic Trends: Atomic and Ionic Radius, Ionization Energy.
Chemical Bonding and Structure (5h)
Cations and Anions; give and take of electrons. Ionic Bond, Covalent Bond: Single, Double and Triple bonds. Bond length and Bond order, Bond Polarity, Lewis Structures, The Octet Rule. The VSEPR theory applied to 4 pair Electron Domains.
Intermolecular Forces. Metallic Bonding (5h)
Electronegativity. Intermolecular Forces (IMF): London (dispersion) forces, dipole-dipole forces and hydrogen bonding. Metallic Bonding.
Energetics / Thermochemistry (5h)
Heat, temperature and Total Energy. Endo and Exothermic Reactions. The Standard Enthalpy Change (H°) . Hess's Law. Change in Internal Energy.
